Meaning of programming

Programming is the writing of a set of commands, or what is known as the code or code, and these commands are what constitute what is known as the program, it is what guides and determines its behavior. A computer or electronic device translates and executes these commands In the programming process, special languages known as programming languages are used. These languages carry vocabulary in addition to specific rules to guide the computer system to carry out specific tasks.
